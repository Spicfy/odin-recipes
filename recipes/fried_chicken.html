Fried chicken

Introduction

In the little village where I was born, life moved at a slower pace, yet felt all the richer for it. There, my two uncles were known far and wide for their delicious cooking. They seasoned their zesty chicken using only the freshest herbs and spices. People call them Los Pollos Hermanos: the chicken brothers. Today we carry on their tradition in a manner that would make my uncles proud. The finest ingredients are brought together with love and care, then slow cooked to perfection. Yes, the old ways are still best at Los Pollos Hermanos. But don’t take my word for it. One taste, and you’ll know.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • vegetable oil for frying

precook

  1. In a large bowl, mix 4 cups of water, 1/4 cup of salt, 1/4 cup of sugar, black peppercorns, smashed arlic cloves, and bay leaves until the sugar and salt dissolve
  2. Add chicken pieces to the brine, ensuring they are fully submerged
  3. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap or a lid, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, up to overnight for the best flavor and tenerness
  4. Remove chicken from brin and pat dry thoroughly with paper towels.
  5. Place chicken pieces in a large bowl and pour buttermilk over them, ensuring each piece is coated
  6. Cover bowl and refrigerate atleast 1 hour up to overnight
  7. Remove chicken from buttermilk and let excess drip off
  8. Season chicken with salt and pepper
  9. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, pepper, paprika, and garlic powder
  10. Coat each piece of chicken in the flour mixture, shaking off excess
  11. Place chicken on a wire rack set over a baking sheet
  12. Let chicken rest for 10 minutes
  13. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat until it reaches 350 degrees Farenheit
  14. Fry chicken in batches, turning occasionally, until golden brown and cooked through, about 15 minutes
  15. Transfer chicken to a wire rack set over a baking sheet to drain
  16. Let chicken rest for 10 minutes before serving
