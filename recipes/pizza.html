Cheese Pizza

Introduction

Indulge in the timeless classic. Our Cheese pizza is a symphony of flavors, starting with a perfectly baked crust topped with a rich tomato sauce and a generous blend of gooey, melted cheeses. Each bite is a delicious reminder of why simplicity is sometimes the most satisfying. Perfect for any cheese lover or those seeking a comforting slice of pizza perfection.

Ingredients

  • 1 ball Pizza dough
    • All-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup basuc Pizza Sauce
  • 3 ounces shredded low-moisture mozzarella
  • Fresh basil or oregano leaves, for garnishing
  • Olive oil
  • minced garlic, slat, pepper
  • Italian herbs

Steps

  1. preheat the oven to 500 Farenheit
  2. Roll out pizza dough to desired thickness. If using store-bought dough, follow package instructions
  3. In a bowl mix together the tomatoes, olive oil, mnced garlic, salt, pepper, and italian herbs
    • Place rolled out dough onto a parchment paper to transfer into the oven
    • Spread a layer of pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
    • Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce
